About Southern Park Home Services Ltd


Southern Park Home Services Ltd is a 2nd generation - family run business with a combined experience of over 20 years. We specialise in all park home related repairs and can cover a large range of your repair needs in East Sussex.


We are based in the South of England in East Sussex. Our team is skilled at all types of park home repairs. We take pride in providing a high-quality service of repairs. Take a look below at some photos of the work we have done.

Why choose us


QUALITY WORK: We are a family run business that have gained decades of experience from working in the park homes business, over the years we have repaired and re-built hundreds of park homes. We know that choosing the right people to repair your park home is a crucial decision, and we want to provide a first-class service to make sure all your needs are met. Our team takes pride in great craftsmanship and customer service. We offer a wide range of repairs from painting the exterior of your park home to double glazing repairs. As a new business, we rely on customer satisfaction that can only come from us providing you with a first-class job.


RANGE OF WORK: We can provide first class repairs at competitive prices. The park home repairs or replacements can include (but not limited to): - double glazing, facias, soffits, kitchens, bathrooms, roofing, floors and doors, and exterior painting. From jacks to roofs we can cover your park home repair needs.
